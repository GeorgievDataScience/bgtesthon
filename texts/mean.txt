This scenario reflects average rent growth, based on the mean of historical changes in rent.
It represents the average yearly increase observed between 1983 and 2025.

This is a balanced scenario, though actual outcomes may vary above or below this level in individual years.

The model uses the Consumer Price Index (CPI) – Rent of Primary Residence, averaging monthly values into yearly figures and calculating year-over-year percentage changes by comparing each year to the previous one.

For example, if the index rises from 100 to 103, this corresponds to a 3% annual increase in rent. This is done for each year to form a full historical distribution.

These results are based on historical data and are not predictions of future rent changes.
